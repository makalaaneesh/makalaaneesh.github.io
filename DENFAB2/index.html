OUR BUSINESS

We extend a broad spectrum of Design and drafting expertise in the fields of Civil & Structural, Mechanical, Piping & Electrical Engineering. We ave expertise in various heavy industrial building structures, architectural and civil works etc.

Structural Engineering

We provide structural engineering services to both private and public sector clients. Our solutions are consistent with the goals of our clients. We research, develop, and present multiple options if appropriate and help to select the best solution. A partner of the firm manages each project. A qualified team of professionals assists them maintaining the highest standard of customer service. Our clients are assured they are dealing with a decision maker who is keenly aware of the importance of success on each and every project no matter the size.

Computer aided design

We offer complete consultancy services from start to end including preparation of sketches, design, concept layout, working drawings, specifications and material schedules, tender documents, assistance in pre-bid documentation, selection of contractors, preparation of PERT and its monitoring, periodic checking of works at site, helping in total co-ordination of works and preparation of completion plans of work to be executed.
