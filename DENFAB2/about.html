VISION AND MISSION

To maintain a world class reputation in all our business activities, founded on the highest standards of customer service , ethics and environmental care.





Denfab at a glance

The Denfab Engineers is leading Consultant working in India and Globally. Our expertise also include retro fittings, revamp of existing projects in cost effective manner. Our consultancy activity develops sustainable solutions. We combined our specialist skills from technical solutions and practical engineering & field experience. We pride ourselves on our competitive combination of project management and technical expertise.

The Denfab Engineers brings you a world of Engineering expertise, in the field of civil, structural, architecture, piping, mechanical, electrical, control & instrumentation for projects in power, steel, cement, glass, refinery, petro-chemicals, alumina, aluminum smelter, lead chemical, gas processing, water intake ,water treatment, sewage treatment plants, metro stations, stadium, infrastructures, multistory and commercial complexes, warehouses etc.

Our main target has been to offer economical and efficient total project consultancy services for civil, structural, architectural, Mechanical & Electrical Engineering .We offer complete consultancy services from start to end including preparation of sketches, design, concept layout, working drawings, specifications and material schedules, tender documents, assistance in pre-bid documentation, selection of contractors, preparation of PERT and its monitoring, periodic checking of works at site, helping in total co-ordination of works and preparation of completion plans of work to be executed.
